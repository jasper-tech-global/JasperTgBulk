ElasticEmail Configuration

Host: smtp.elasticemail.com

Port: 587 (STARTTLS) or 465 (TLS)

Username: Your ElasticEmail login email

Password: Your ElasticEmail API key

Security: Enable STARTTLS for port 587, or TLS for port 465

💡 Pro Tip: Port 587 with STARTTLS is recommended for better compatibility and security.

Gmail Configuration

Host: smtp.gmail.com

Port: 587 (STARTTLS)

Username: Your Gmail address

Password: App password (not regular password)

Security: Enable STARTTLS

🔐 Security: You need to generate an App Password in your Google Account settings.

Outlook Configuration

Host: smtp-mail.outlook.com

Port: 587 (STARTTLS)

Username: Your Outlook email address

Password: Your Outlook password

Security: Enable STARTTLS

⚠️ Note: You may need to enable "Less secure app access" in your Outlook account.

SendGrid Configuration

Host: smtp.sendgrid.net

Port: 587 (STARTTLS) or 465 (TLS)

Username: apikey

Password: Your SendGrid API key

Security: Enable STARTTLS for port 587, or TLS for port 465

🔑 Username: Always use "apikey" as the username with SendGrid.
